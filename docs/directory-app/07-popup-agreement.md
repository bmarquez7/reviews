# Popup Agreement

Modal title: Before you continue

Modal body rules:
1) Firsthand experiences only. Post only what happened to you personally.
2) Keep it professional and specific. No insults, harassment, threats, hate speech, or discriminatory content.
3) No private information. Do not share emails, phone numbers, home addresses, or identifying details about individuals.
4) Moderation and enforcement. We may remove content that breaks these rules, and we may suspend accounts for repeated violations.
